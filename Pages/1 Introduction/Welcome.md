## What is SkyRoam?

**SkyRoam** is a Spatial Computing Engine framework.

## Why use SkyRoam?

Built with native platform tools, SkyRoam provides everything you need to succeed.

### Multi-Engine Compatibility
Unified interface supports multiple engines, enabling operations without additional development or integration

### Elastic Scalability
Seamlessly adapts to small, medium, and large-scale applications with consistent performance

### High Extensibility
Flexible expansion support for both engine and service sides to meet diverse needs

### Zero-Configuration Deployment
Extremely simplified, ready to use out-of-the-box with no complex setup

### Community-Driven Development
Iteration fueled by real-world projects, with practical experience feedback contributing to optimal community solutions

### Open Source & Free
Fully open-sourced with zero cost, empowering content development
