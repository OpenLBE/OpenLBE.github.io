How to deploy with SkyRoam.

Now, take the PICO 4 Ultra Enterprise device as an example
Enter "Settings - About This Device", find "Software Version", click 8 times with the cursor aligned until "Developer Options" appears below the left navigation bar; click to enter "Developer", and then enter "Enterprise Settings"; 
Turn on the large space mode and create a map. You can refer to the official link
